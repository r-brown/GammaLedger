<p>Welcome to GammaLedger! This guide will help you get started with tracking and analyzing your options portfolio.</p>
<h2 id="what-is-gammaledger">What is GammaLedger?</h2>
<p>GammaLedger is a powerful, privacy-focused options trading analytics platform that runs entirely in your browser. All your data stays on your device—no servers, no tracking, complete control.</p>
<h2 id="quick-start">Quick Start</h2>
<h3 id="1-launch-the-application">1. Launch the Application</h3>
<p>Simply navigate to <a href="https://gammaledger.com/app/">GammaLedger App</a> and the application will load in your browser.</p>
<h3 id="2-import-your-trade-data">2. Import Your Trade Data</h3>
<p>GammaLedger supports importing trade data from popular brokers:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>TD Ameritrade/Schwab</strong>: Export your trades as CSV</li>
<li><strong>Interactive Brokers</strong>: Use the Flex Query system</li>
<li><strong>E*TRADE</strong>: Export transaction history</li>
<li><strong>Manual Entry</strong>: Add trades one by one</li>
</ul>
<p>To import data:
1. Click the <strong>Import</strong> button in the top navigation
2. Select your broker format
3. Upload your CSV or JSON file
4. Review and confirm the imported trades</p>
<h3 id="3-explore-your-dashboard">3. Explore Your Dashboard</h3>
<p>Once your trades are imported, you'll see:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Active Positions</strong>: Current open trades with real-time metrics</li>
<li><strong>Performance Analytics</strong>: P&amp;L charts, win rates, and strategy breakdown</li>
<li><strong>Trade History</strong>: Complete audit trail of all your trades</li>
<li><strong>Strategy Trackers</strong>: Specialized views for Wheel, PMCC, and other strategies</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="key-features">Key Features</h2>
<h3 id="real-time-quote-integration">Real-Time Quote Integration</h3>
<p>GammaLedger integrates with Finnhub to provide real-time market data:</p>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Settings</strong> → <strong>API Configuration</strong></li>
<li>Enter your Finnhub API key (free tier available)</li>
<li>Enable auto-refresh for live updates</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="strategy-tracking">Strategy Tracking</h3>
<p>GammaLedger automatically categorizes your trades:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Wheel Strategy</strong>: Cash-secured puts rolling into covered calls</li>
<li><strong>PMCC (Poor Man's Covered Call)</strong>: LEAPS-based diagonal spreads</li>
<li><strong>Credit Spreads</strong>: Vertical spreads for defined risk</li>
<li><strong>Iron Condors</strong>: Range-bound strategies</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="privacy-first">Privacy First</h3>
<p>All calculations happen in your browser:
- ✅ No data sent to external servers
- ✅ localStorage for persistence
- ✅ Export/backup your data anytime
- ✅ Open source and transparent</p>
<h2 id="pro-tips">Pro Tips</h2>
<h3 id="backup-your-data">Backup Your Data</h3>
<p>Regularly export your data:
1. Click <strong>Settings</strong> → <strong>Export Data</strong>
2. Save the JSON file to a secure location
3. Use version control (optional but recommended)</p>
<h3 id="customize-your-views">Customize Your Views</h3>
<ul>
<li>Adjust column visibility in tables</li>
<li>Set custom date ranges for analytics</li>
<li>Configure profit/loss display preferences</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="track-multiple-accounts">Track Multiple Accounts</h3>
<p>Use the account grouping feature to separate:
- Different brokers
- Tax-advantaged vs taxable accounts
- Paper trading vs live trading</p>
